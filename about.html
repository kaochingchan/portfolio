Bio

高慶展2024年畢業於臺藝視傳。從在學期間起便專注於研究漢字設計，曾獲得Justfont第六屆造字鼓勵元首獎，以職業字體設計師為目標努力，業餘期間亦從事標準字設計，持續保持創作活力。


除了字體設計外，對於UIUX領域也有所涉略，透過黑客松、App Redesign等Side Project的參與累積經驗，同時具有初階前端開發能力，參與部分專案網頁的介面規劃/開發，發揮設計、開發兩項能力，形塑出更好的設計、用戶體驗。



ChingChan Kao graduated from the Visual Communication Design apartment at National Taiwan University of the Arts in 2024. Since his student days, he has focused on the study of Hanzi design and won the Encouragement Award at the 6th Justfont Type Design Competition. Aiming to become a professional type designer, he also dabbles in logotype design in his spare time to maintain his creative vigour.


In addition to type design, Kao has also ventured into the field of UI/UX. By participating in hackathons and app redesign side projects, he has gained valuable experience. Possessing basic front-end development skills, he has contributed to the interface planning and development of several projects, leveraging both design and development skills to create better design and user experiences.
